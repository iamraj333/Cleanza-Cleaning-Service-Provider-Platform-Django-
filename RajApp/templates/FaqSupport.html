Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

If you forget your password or want to secure your account with a new one, start by visiting the login page and clicking the “Forgot Password” link. You will be prompted to enter the email address associated with your account. Shortly after, you will receive an email containing a secure link to reset your password. Make sure to choose a strong, unique password that combines letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays or common words to ensure your account remains safe.
Our support team is dedicated to helping you with any issues or questions you may have. You can reach us through our Contact Support page, where you can submit a detailed request describing your problem. We recommend including screenshots or detailed steps to help us respond more efficiently. Our average response time is within 24 hours, though more complex inquiries may take slightly longer. Support is available for account issues, billing questions, technical troubleshooting, and guidance on features.
Our complete documentation is available on the Documentation page. It includes step-by-step guides, video tutorials, FAQs, and troubleshooting tips for all features. We recommend reviewing the relevant sections for your use case, whether you are a new user setting up your account or an advanced user exploring advanced functionality. Keeping the documentation bookmarked is helpful for quick reference.
We accept major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express, as well as PayPal. All payments are processed securely with encryption to ensure your billing information is safe. For subscription plans, payments are automatically billed at the start of each billing cycle. If your card expires or a payment fails, you will receive notifications to update your information to avoid service interruptions. Keep your payment method up to date to maintain uninterrupted access.
To update personal information such as your name, email address, or password, log in to your account and navigate to the Profile Settings section. From there, you can edit your details securely. After making changes, ensure you save your updates and, if necessary, verify your email address. Updating your account information promptly helps keep your profile accurate and ensures you receive notifications and billing information correctly.
Yes, your data security is our top priority. We employ industry-standard encryption protocols for data transmission and storage, including SSL for all communications. Access to sensitive information is strictly controlled, and we perform regular audits and security updates to prevent unauthorized access. We also recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) for additional protection. With these measures, your personal and financial data remains secure while using our platform.

For urgent problems, submitting a detailed support request with screenshots or error messages can help us resolve your issue faster. Upgrading your subscription is easy. Log in to your account and navigate to the Subscription section of your dashboard. Select the desired plan that fits your needs and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the upgrade. Any changes to billing, features, or storage limits will take effect immediately or at the start of the next billing cycle. Make sure to review the plan details, pricing, and benefits before confirming your upgrade.
